Methodology

How we calculate prosperity scores

Scoring System

Each pillar is scored on a 0–100 scale, where 100 represents the best observed performance globally. Scores are derived from multiple sub-indicators normalized to this scale.

Data Sources

We aggregate data from the following international organizations and databases:

  • World Bank Open Data
  • United Nations Data
  • Freedom House
  • V-Dem Institute
  • World Health Organization
  • International Energy Agency
  • International Labour Organization
  • WIPO Global Innovation Index

Update Frequency

Scores are updated as new data becomes available from source organizations, typically on an annual basis. The platform reflects the most recent available data.

Limitations

No single index can fully capture the complexity of national prosperity. Our scores should be viewed as approximate indicators based on available data, not definitive rankings.
